Highlife

Nationwide

The quintessential Ghanaian genre that blends traditional rhythms with Western instruments. Originating in the early 20th century, Highlife became the soundtrack of Ghana's independence era.

Hiplife

Urban Centers

A fusion genre combining Highlife with hip hop that emerged in the 1990s. Hiplife became the dominant urban sound of Ghana, with artists rapping in local languages over contemporary beats.
